About TheraNest

We have a simple mission: to create affordable, yet impactful software applications to help mental health professionals, and social service organizations succeed at what they do - improving outcomes for the clients they serve.

Our mission was born out of a need we saw in our local community that then inspired us to create something to fill the need - great tools for social workers, therapists, psychotherapists, and other mental health professionals and their clients that are modern, updated quickly and affordable.

We believe that Better Tools can enhance Better Treatments and lead to Better Outcomes
