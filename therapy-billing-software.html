Other TheraNest Features

Robust Therapy Notes

TheraNest offers you both DSM-IV and DSM 5 ready notes with diagnostic codes available and easy to pre-select. Easily create treatment plans with treatment goals and objectives - select from existing MSE choices or add yours in your notes.

TheraBook is our easy to use client portal add-on for scheduling and intake forms. Let clients prefill demographic and payment information before their first session or at any time.
