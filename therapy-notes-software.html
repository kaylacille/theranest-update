Therapy Notes Software for Mental and Behavioral Health Professionals

TheraNest helps you easily manage your mental health practice.

Easy to use therapy notes software for mental health professionals

  • Create individual and group notes
  • Therapy notes templates for initial assessments, progress notes, treatment plans and discharge
  • Easily search and select DSM codes (IV and 5)
  • Approve and co-sign notes
