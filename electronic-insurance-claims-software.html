Electronic Insurance Claims Software

TheraNest is a cloud-based HIPAA compliant practice management and case notes software with a built-in electronic claims feature. TheraNest is used by solo practices, small practices, non-profits, as well as large organizations.

Easy Invoicing

TheraNest makes it easy for you to create, print, and email invoices and superbills - never worry about you or your practice forgetting to bill for a session.

Analytics/Reporting

Keep track of invoice and billing reports, delinquent progress notes, referral sources and more!

Unlimited User Accounts

No matter your practice size, TheraNest fits your needs. From sole practitioners to large mental health practices and non-profits and everything in between.
