Social Services Software

TheraNest is designed for clinical social workers and priced to be affordable for social service agencies of all sizes.

In Depth Therapy Notes

Progress notes, treatment plans, discharge notes, and DSM-5 ready. Easy to use, auto-filled mental status assessments. Upload unlimited client files and documents.

Analytics/Reporting

Keep track of invoices and billing reports, delinquent progress notes, referral sources and more!

Unlimited Staff & User Accounts

No matter your practice size, TheraNest fits your needs. From sole practitioners to large mental health practices and non-profits and everything in between.

Secure and Cloud Based

HIPAA compliant - data is backed up multiple times a day and encrypted using bank level SSL certificates when you log in. Notes are web based and accessible anywhere!

Appointment Reminder Feature

TheraNest includes appointment reminders via voice calls, email or SMS text. No set-up or contracts necessary. Turn on/off as needed.

Conduct Business Easily

Accept credit card payments directly through TheraNest.
