Human Services Software for Better Outcomes

TheraNest ha a simple mission: to create affordable, yet impactful software applications to help mental health professionals, and social service organizations succeed at what they do - improving outcomes for the clients they serve. TheraNest is used by practices and organizations of all sizes: sole practitioners, large mental health and psychology practices, social services non-profits, and educational and teaching centers.

Easy Invoicing

TheraNest makes it easy for you to create, print, and email invoices and superbills - never worry about you or your practice forgetting to bill for a session.

Analytics/Reporting

Keep track of invoice and billing reports, delinquent progress notes, referral sources and more!

Unlimited User Accounts

No matter your practice size, TheraNest fits your needs. From sole practitioners to large mental health practices and non-profits and everything in between.
