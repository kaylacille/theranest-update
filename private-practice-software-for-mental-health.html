Private Practice Software for Mental Health

TheraNest is a web-based HIPAA compliant practice management software designed for your private practice. It is the ideal EMR and practice management software designed for organizations of any size - from solo practitioners to large practices.

  • Easy therapy notes software
  • HIPAA compliant - data is backed up multiple times a day and encrypted using bank level SSL certificates when you log in
  • Notes are secure and web based and are accessible anywhere
  • TheraNest includes appointment reminders via voice calls, email or SMS text
  • Accept credit card payments right from inside the software
  • Print superbills and generate CMS-1500 (HCFA) forms
  • Keep your appointments organized with our full-featured calendar
