Robust Therapy Notes

TheraNest offers you both DSM-IV and DSM 5 ready notes with diagnostic codes available and easy to pre-select. Easily create treatment plans with treatment goals and objectives - select from existing MSE choices or add yours in your notes.

Easy Invoicing

TheraNest makes it easy for you to create, print, and email invoices and superbills - never worry about you or your practice forgetting to bill for a session.

Unlimited User Accounts

No matter your practice type or size, TheraNest fits your needs. Add as many therapists, billers, and staff as you want at no additional charge. We grow with you as your client base grows.

Comprehensive Dashboard

Increase productivity within your practice by centralizing the essentials. Theranest dashboard brings all of your appointments, invoices and clients all in one place.

Full-featured Calendar

Keep your appointments organized - view from your desktop, tablet or mobile phone. Track cancellations and no-shows with a single click.

Appointment Reminder Feature

TheraNest includes appointment reminders via voice calls, email or SMS text. No set-up or contracts necessary. Turn on/off as needed.

Analytics/Reporting

An in-depth look at how your practice ticks. Keep track of invoice and billing reports, delinquent progress notes, referral sources and more!

Unmatched Customer Support

Fast, friendly, and most importantly, helpful, we will stop at nothing to provide top-tier support. Chat with us live right within the application - ask us anything or share your feedback.

Client Portal Add-On

TheraBook is our easy to use client portal add-on for scheduling and intake forms. Design your own therapy intake forms or use our pre-built templates.
