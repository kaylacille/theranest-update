Client Portal Add-On

TheraBook is our easy to use client portal add-on for scheduling and intake forms. Design your own therapy intake forms or use our pre-built templates.

  • Clients can submit and update demographic information from the portal
  • Clients can fill out, sign and submit intake forms online
  • TheraBook allows you to design your own therapy intake form or use our pre-built templates
  • Select which clients have access to the portal
  • Select staff members who can be scheduled through the portal
